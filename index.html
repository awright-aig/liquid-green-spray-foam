About Us

Liquid Green Spray Foam Insulation

We have created many strong believers in foam insulation throughout our years in business. Commercial building owners as well as homeowners throughout the tri-state area are reaping the benefits of being built with spray foam insulation. Most builders that we work with today tell us that they continue to use foam because they have seen it makes a difference. They know the value that it adds to a structure, and the comfort and savings it will provide for years to come.

Spray foam insulation in both commercial and residential construction offers both immediate and long term benefits. It can lower your heating and cooling costs by up to 50%, reduce the chance of mold growth, discourage infestation of household pests, improve air quality and protect against respiratory problems by reducing allergens, pollutants, and pollen. Spray foam insulation will also increase the resale value of your home, and even reduce external noise pollution. These short and long term benefits far outweigh the cost of initial installation and will keep you and your family comfortable in your home for many years to come.

Benefits

Benefits of Liquid Green Spray Foam Over Conventional Insulation

Superior Thermal Performance

Spray foam insulation provides the highest R-value per inch of any insulation material, keeping your home warmer in winter and cooler in summer.

Optimal Air Tightening

Unlike fiberglass, spray foam expands to fill every gap and crack, creating an airtight seal that stops drafts and energy loss.

Enhanced Sound Control

Spray foam insulation significantly reduces noise transmission between rooms and from outside, creating a quieter living environment.

Moisture Resistant

Liquid Green spray foam far exceeds fiberglass or cellulose in its moisture resistant properties and helps prevent mold growth.

Class 1 Fire Rating

Our spray foam insulation is not a fuel source and offers a class 1 fire rating for your safety and peace of mind.

No Food for Pests

Liquid Green spray foam has zero food value for rodents and insects, discouraging infestation of household pests.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is spray foam insulation Green?

Spray foam insulation acts as a fundamental building block of Green building by preserving the heat and cool air you paid to generate, reducing your carbon footprint. So, yes, Liquid Green spray foam insulation is Green!

Is spray foam insulation flammable?

Liquid Green spray foam insulation is not a fuel source, and offers a class 1 fire rating.

Is spray foam insulation moisture resistant?

Liquid Green spray foam insulation far exceeds fiberglass or cellulose in its moisture resistant properties. It helps prevent airborne moisture across the building envelope, one of the main causes of mold. You could call it hydro-phobic!

Is spray foam insulation a food source for rodents or insects?

No! Liquid Green spray foam insulation has zero food value for rodents and insects.

What are the additional benefits of spray foam?
