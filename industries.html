FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the industries and project types Liquid Green serves.

Do you service residential and commercial projects?

Yes. Liquid Green serves both residential and commercial clients throughout the tri-state area. Our mobile crews handle projects of every size, from single-family homes to large commercial buildings.

Do you insulate new construction or only retrofits?

We do both. For new construction, spray foam is installed before drywall to create a continuous air barrier from day one. For retrofits and renovations, we can inject foam into existing wall cavities or apply it to accessible spaces like attics, basements, and rim joists.

Do you serve agricultural buildings?

Yes. We insulate barns, outbuildings, and agricultural structures, helping regulate temperature, reduce condensation, and improve conditions for equipment, livestock, and stored materials.

Do you only work in the tri-state area?

Our primary service area is the tri-state region — New York, Connecticut, and New Jersey. Contact us to confirm availability for your specific location; we're happy to discuss projects outside this area on a case-by-case basis.

Do you insulate metal buildings?

Yes. Spray foam is one of the best insulation choices for metal buildings because it eliminates the condensation issues that come with metal construction and provides a seamless thermal envelope. We handle metal building projects of all sizes.
