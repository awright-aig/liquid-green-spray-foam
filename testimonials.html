FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our work and customer experience.

Where can I see examples of Liquid Green's work?

Beyond this page, we maintain a project gallery on our home page featuring attic insulation, residential homes, commercial projects, and more. Our Facebook and Instagram pages also include recent installation photos.

What types of projects has Liquid Green completed?

We've completed hundreds of projects across the tri-state area, including residential homes, commercial buildings, agricultural structures, renovations, and new construction. Every project is installed by our trained, professional crews.

How do I leave a review for Liquid Green?

We love hearing from customers. You can leave a review on our Facebook page, our Yelp listing, or by emailing us directly at info@sprayliquidgreen.com. We read every piece of feedback and use it to keep getting better.

Can I get a reference from a past Liquid Green customer?

Yes. We're happy to connect you with past customers in your area who have had similar work done. Just ask during your estimate and we'll arrange an introduction.
