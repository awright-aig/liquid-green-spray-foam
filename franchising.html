FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about owning a Liquid Green franchise.

How much does a Liquid Green franchise cost?

Startup investment for a Liquid Green franchise ranges from $136,000 to $194,000 — significantly lower than major insulation franchises that often exceed $200,000 to $500,000. The total covers equipment, training, initial inventory, and launch support.

What is the target revenue for a Liquid Green franchise?

Our model targets revenue potential of $500,000 within 3-5 years, supported by a protected territory model that lets you grow market share without competing with other franchisees.

Do I get a protected territory with a Liquid Green franchise?

Yes. Each Liquid Green franchisee is assigned a protected territory, giving you exclusive rights to operate within a defined geographic area. This is a core part of the business model and supports long-term growth.

Do I need spray foam experience to open a Liquid Green franchise?

No prior spray foam experience is required. We provide comprehensive training on equipment operation, application technique, sales, and business operations. The mobile business model is designed to be run by motivated operators, not necessarily insulation veterans.

What support does Liquid Green provide franchisees?

Liquid Green franchisees receive comprehensive training, a CRM for franchise management, ongoing operational support, brand guidelines, and a proven playbook. You're in business for yourself, but not by yourself.

Can I open multiple Liquid Green franchise territories?

Yes. The model is built for scalability. Multi-territory ownership is available for operators who want to grow a larger regional business over time, subject to territory availability and performance.
